Feed The Children

Feed the Children is an anti-hunger organization. It envisions a world where no child goes to bed hungry. It distributes food to various places and gives support to kids through others means like classroom support and community development programs.

It has distributed 98.9 million pounds of food and reached more than eight million children and families. It has benefitted more than 2 million people in international communities, helped close to 30 thousand teachers, and provided more than 2.1 million pounds of food and supplies. It helped a lot of people which is why we consider it a great organization that you should try to support.
