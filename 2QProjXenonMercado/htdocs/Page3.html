Save The Children

Save the Children is an organization dedicated to helping children. They help in emergencies, speak up for children's rights, and they create better lives for children in the Philippines and across the world.

Eglantyne Jebb, an Oxford-educated teacher and sociologist, is the person that started it all. She believed that children have the right to a healthy, happy, and fulfilling life, no matter who they are or where they come from. They have been present in the Philippines for nearly 40 years, being present since 1981. They provide education, child protection, disaster risk reduction, etc.
